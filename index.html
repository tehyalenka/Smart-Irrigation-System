SMART IRRIGATION SYSTEM

Automation is what helps your orchard business grow steady, year by year. And it gets you your time and money back.

The smart irrigation watering system is a mechanism for watering plants automatically It's objective is to make gardening hassle-free. The goal of this project is to automate the irrigation system and plant monitoring.

We are aware that plants are the saviours of our environment. By organically purifying the air and releasing oxygen, plants contribute to a healthy atmosphere. The presence of plants in the backyard is quite popular. But providing adequate water to them every single day is a difficult task. Knowing the quantity of water requires and the right time to water the plants are two crucial components of the gardening. People frequently forget to water their plants owing to their hectic daily schedules, which lets plants to die eventually. In order to do this task automatically, a system is needed. Intelligent irrigation systems provide the desired amount of water that the plant needs after automatically estimating and measuring the humidity of plants. In addition to maintaining the health of the plants, it reduces excessive water usage.
